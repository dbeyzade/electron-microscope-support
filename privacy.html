Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Electron Microscope (macOS) handles information. We designed the app for local analysis workflows and minimal data collection.

Effective date: March 25, 2026 | Owner contact: dbeyzade@hotmail.com

1. Scope

This policy applies to Electron Microscope for macOS, including support interactions and App Store distribution workflows.

2. Information We Process

Data processed locally on your Mac

  • Scientific/analysis input files you open in the app
  • Generated images and analysis results
  • Local app preferences and local access counters

Data shared only when you choose

  • Email content you send to support
  • Attachments/logs/screenshots you provide voluntarily
  • Issue details posted by you on GitHub
No automatic upload of analysis files by default

3. Payments and Subscriptions

Subscription and purchase transactions are processed through Apple systems. We do not directly collect or store your full payment card data. For transaction handling and billing data practices, please refer to Apple’s policies.

4. How We Use Information

  • To provide app functionality and local analysis features
  • To respond to support or privacy requests
  • To diagnose bugs from information you explicitly share
  • To improve quality, stability, and user safety

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We do not share your analysis files unless you intentionally send them to support. Third-party processing may occur only through platforms you choose to use (for example, Apple App Store or email providers).

6. Data Retention

Local files remain on your device unless you remove them. Support emails and issue reports are retained as needed to provide support, maintain service quality, and satisfy legal obligations.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect support communications and operational data. No method of storage or transmission is completely risk-free, but we continuously improve safeguards.

8. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can request deletion of support emails you sent, where legally permissible.
  • You can avoid sharing personal data by not attaching sensitive files to support messages.
  • You can contact us to ask privacy questions at any time.

9. Children’s Privacy

Electron Microscope is not intentionally directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact: dbeyzade@hotmail.com

11. Policy Updates

We may update this policy to reflect product changes, legal requirements, or support improvements. Updated versions will be published on this page with a new effective date.
